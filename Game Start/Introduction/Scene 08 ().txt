#简体 version
公寓里依然空无一人。
-No one is in the apartment block.

屋里屋外绕了一趟，谁都没有看见。
-I checked both inside and outside, and did not see anyone.

不过我却在公寓的小小庭院里看见了房东。
-But I did see the landlord in the apartment block’s small garden.

虽然住进来的时候，就知道房东有画具。
但实际看她画图，这还是头一回。
-When I moved in I was aware that the landlord owned drawing supplies.
-But this is the first time I have actually seen her drawing.

她专心地拿着笔，对着素描簿，聚精会神地画着些什么进去。
一笔一画，都散发着令人安心的宁静感。
-She is holding her pencil attentively, concentrating fully on drawing something in her sketchbook.
-Each pencil stroke radiates a soothing aura of tranquillity.

这或许就是师傅说的“寂”、“幽”、“玄”的境界。
但也可能只是因为我们公寓的院子里，吹的风太沁人心脾。
-Perhaps this is what the master meant by the state of stillness, serenity and wisdom.
-But then, maybe it’s just because the wind in the apartment block’s garden is so refreshing.

林明华：
。。。。。。你在看什么？
-… What are you looking at?

被发现了。
-I’ve been noticed.

李肇维：
呃。。。。。。我在看妳。
-Err… I’m looking at you.

发现到我的存在，她很快地将写生簿阖上，瞪视着我。
-Upon noticing my presence, she quickly closes the sketchbook and glares at me.

林明华：
你你你你从什么时候开始看的！看了多久！为什么都不说！
-W… Wh… When did you start looking?! How long were you looking?! Why didn’t you say anything?!

李肇维：
我才刚回来嘛。
-I only just got back.

林明华：
你没偷看我画了什么吧！
-You didn’t peek at what I was drawing, did you?!

李肇维：
我除了妳之外，什么都没看到。
-Apart from you, I didn’t see anything.

林明华：
除了我之外。。。。。。
-Apart from me…

房东的脸涨的更红了。
-The landlord’s face becomes even redder.

林明华：
哼。。。。。。哼！就算你这么说，我也不会少收你房租的！
-Humph… humph! Even if you say such things, I won’t reduce your rent!

李肇维：
早上妳已经收过了不是吗？
-Didn’t you already collect it this morning?

林明华：
难道你这个月交过租，下个月就不用了吗？
-Do you think that having paid rent this month, you won’t need to pay next month?

李肇维：
学校老师告诉过，小便宜贪不得。不过我没想到，房东妳居然真的会画图呢。
-My school teacher once said to not covet petty gains. But I never expected that you really knew how to draw.

林明华：
那还用说，我以前也是科班的学生，一天要画上好几张，手可热的呢。不像现在，心情好了才有力气撇上几笔。
-That goes without saying. I used to be a performing arts student. Every day I would have to draw several pictures. Unlike now, I only draw when my mood is good.
 
李肇维：
因为妳现在是房东了嘛。
-Because now you’re a landlord.

林明华：
不，是因为我毕业了。
-No, it’s because I’ve graduated.

李肇维：
啊哈哈，我跟元德都没毕业呢。
-Ahaha, Yuan De and I never graduated.

林明华：
为什么？
-Why?

李肇维：
什么为什么？
-Why what?

林明华：
你们两个为什么都没毕业？
-Why didn’t you two graduate?

李肇维：
那个啊，是因为。。。。。。
-Oh, that, that’s because…

因为战争。
-Because of the war.

林明华：
。。。。。。
-…

房东慢慢的放下手上的画板。
-The landlord slowly puts down the sketchbook she was holding. 

李肇维：
妳不继续画吗？
-You’re not going to continue drawing?

林明华：
不画了，没心情。
-No, I’m not in the mood.

房东往我看了一眼。
-The landlord looks at me.

林明华：
而且给你盯着，哪画的下去。
-What’s more you were staring at me, how could I continue drawing?

李肇维：
那可真是对不起了。。。。。。
-I’m really sorry about that…

林明华：
那么，为了展现你的对不起——
-In that case, to show that you are sorry --

房东先看了看左右，不用看了这里没人。
-The landlord looks around. There’s no need to look because there’s no one here.

林明华：
好，叔不在。
-Good, my uncle isn’t here.

房东深吸了几口气。
-The landlord takes a few deep breaths.

林明华：
要不要跟我一起出去吃饭？
-Would you go out with me for dinner?

李肇维：
好啊。
-OK.

林明华：
还有下次不要再偷看。
-And don’t peek again.

李肇维：
押忍！
-Yes, Madam!

林明华：
。。。。。。笨蛋。
-… Idiot.
